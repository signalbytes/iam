How to choose

How to pick the right AI tool without wasting weeks

Start with the task, not the brand

If your real problem is turning messy notes into a clear plan, a scheduling and drafting workflow matters more than whichever chatbot is most talked about this week.

Assume every draft needs review

AI is strongest at speed, structure, and first-pass synthesis. It is weaker when accuracy, nuance, citations, or original judgment matter most.

Favor tools with workflow context

The most useful systems are the ones that can keep instructions, files, and iterative work together instead of forcing you to restart from scratch every time.
